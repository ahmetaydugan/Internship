## Day 1: 

I made a productive start to the first day of my internship. Today, I mainly focused on perfecting my working environment and gaining an introduction to the fundamentals of artificial intelligence from a historical perspective.

**1. Setting Up the Working Environment**
I started the day by setting up my technical infrastructure. I installed Python and configured VS Code as my main development environment. To keep my project dependencies isolated and well-organized, I also completed the setup of virtualenv. Starting with a clean and isolated workspace means preventing potential version conflicts in future complex projects, which gives me confidence.

**2. GitHub Repository and First Commit**
With the guidance of my mentor, I created this repository, which will serve as the central hub for all my work during the internship. I prepared a comprehensive README file summarizing the purpose of the repository, my workflow, and the rules I will follow, and made my first commit. Being able to look back and see a clean code history is very important to me.

**3. Academic Block: A Journey to the Roots of Artificial Intelligence**
As the theoretical task of the day, I read the introduction and early sections of the “History of Artificial Intelligence” article on Wikipedia. Honestly, realizing that artificial intelligence is not just a rising star of the last decade, but a field whose theoretical foundations date back to early discussions on logic and computability, was eye-opening. The great vision and passion of early researchers to model human intelligence, despite the limited hardware of their time, is truly impressive. I feel that not only writing code but also understanding the historical background and philosophy of this field will deepen my perspective on the models I will build in the coming weeks.
